STORY ABOUT US

Our journey in plantation and beginning of a new chapter
We Started

In 2019, We took step towards to make healthy and safe Eco Environment in the different fields, and our passion is: to clean the Eco Environment for the next generations who shall face the problem in future.

Aim

To beautify the areas for scenic beauty. To reduce the surface run-off discharge and checking soil erosion along the embankments. To reduce temperature and increase humidity. To reduce noise pollution to the neighboring household population

Objective

This organisation organises tree plantation activities and inculcates the sense of responsibility among the young students across Maharashtra. The name of this program is 'Go Green Kids. ' They provide free saplings in schools and let the students plant and take care of those saplings.

Vision

Education Motivational Program working towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through tree plantation, urban Rural biodiversity conservation, water bodies restoration, sustainable solutions, and environmental research and awareness.

Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable development and preserve the natural environment through community engagement, education, and by implementing innovative, eco-friendly initiatives that create a harmonious balance between human progress and the protection of our planet.

Aspiration

We aim to inspire positive change, foster environmental stewardship, and ensure a better future for generations to come.
